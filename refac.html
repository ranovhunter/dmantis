Evolving with you

About us

At Younexa, our customers always come first. 
And evolving with them is our way of showing them that. 

We are committed to innovation that makes sense, applied to frits, glazes and ceramics. Offering the real needs of our customers concrete solutions thanks to our knowledge and the support of a great group like Altadia, which allows us to take on any challenge.

Our 100 years of history has made us into the dedicated, expert and contemporary partner that we are today, one that is by its customers’ side at all times, through any adventure.

When it comes to developing, we deliver

For over 100 years, we have been skilfully and ceaselessly developing solutions and products that have been recognised by both customers and the industry.

We drive innovation that makes sense

Our innovation always responds to a specific need, the result of meeting the requirements of each market and customer.

We create ties with customers

We are by our customers' side at all times, creating lasting relationships and offering a comprehensive service.

We are part of of Altadia

We have the backing of Altadia, a leading group in the ceramics sector. With the solvency and capability to face the challenges of the future.

We provide our customers with the support they need so they can do their best work. And we know that the only way we can do this is to stay permanently up to date. Just like we’ve always done.
